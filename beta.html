How Testing Works (You Will Not Be Charged)

Our testing process uses the official Google Play "License Testing" system. This allows you to test all features of our apps, including in-app purchases for "Pro" versions.

IMPORTANT: When you test a purchase, you will see the normal Google Play payment screen with the full price. However, because you are an authorized license tester, a message will clearly state: "This is a test purchase, you will not be charged." Your payment method will not be billed.

This allows you to safely and completely test the entire app, including all premium features, at no cost.

Current Test: Spooky Blood Drips

Our first app available for testing is "Spooky Blood Drips." We ask that you please:

  1. Accept the testing invitation you receive via email.

  3. Test the "Pro" upgrade purchase flow.
  4. Explore all the "Pro Settings" (colors, speed, jagged drips, etc.).
  5. Use the app as your default screensaver for a few days.
  6. Provide any feedback or bug reports by replying to your invitation email.
